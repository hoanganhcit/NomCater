Frequently Asked Questions

Is Nomcater available in my city?

Nomcater is currently available in Toronto and the wider GTA area, as well as Calgary, Kitchener/Waterloo and the Greater Vancouver Area.

How much does NomCater cost?

You pay for the food that you order - so your meal plan would completely depend on your budget and the frequency of ordering.

When is my deadline to order from NomCater?

10:30am is your deadline to order lunch regularly. If you're ordering breakfast or dinner the deadline will be 2 hours before your desired delivery time.

Does NomCater have an app?

Yes, employees at offices that have NomCater enabled can use the apps available for iOS and Android or directly on NomCater's website.

Can I order individual meals from NomCater?

Yes, you can! If you're providing your team with regular lunches, empower them with the flexibility and satisfaction of ordering individually boxed meals from the city's best restaurants.

Can I order a catered event from any restaurant?

Yes, you can! If you're ordering for a one-off event or meeting, you can choose from a list of your city's best vendors and select what and when you'd like to order.
